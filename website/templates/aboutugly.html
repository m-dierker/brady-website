About Me

Hi there! My name is Braedon Salz. Call me Brady.

I am currently finishing my senior year at the University of Illinois at Urbana-Champaign.
I plan on receiving my B.S. in Electrical Engineering in May 2015, then attending graduate school.

In the past, I've interned at IBM, Cirrus Logic, and Qualcomm.
I'll be returning to Austin, TX this summer to intern once more at Cirrus.

I love circuit design - from system to board to transistor level. Unfortunately, a lot of the references for circuits are less than ideal - there's no true digital home for an analog world. People will be more than happy to recommend random textbooks (TAoE anyone?), but those aren't sufficient I feel. I intend to sporadically post circuits that I learn about here, breaking down and explaining them. I hope you'll find it as useful as a reference as I will!
